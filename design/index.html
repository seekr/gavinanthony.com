What is design?

Design - a skillful blend of the elements and principles of art - results when all of the parts hold together to produce the best possible effect.

Without this overall principle of unity, the work would "fall apart" or appear disorganized and confusing to the view.

In order to understand the aesthetics of design, you need to know how the principles of art are used. Learning the principles of art will help you recognize and enjoy one of the most fascinating things about works of art: how they are put together.
