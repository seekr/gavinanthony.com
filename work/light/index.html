About Light

Light - Share Effortlessly. Share photos effortlessly to a fast, fun timeline of the last 60 minutes. An application for fast sharing, fun, inspiration, chat or even love. Follow friends, family, celebrities, and interests.

The idea behind the application is very simple. To let users take pictures that last for 60 minutes and to view their friends pictures or to view the most popular pictures through a discovery page. I was the main designer, in charge of designing the UI and UX for the app.
