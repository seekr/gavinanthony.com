About Beekeeper

During my 2014 summer internship at the startup-accelerator company, gener8tor, I worked with BeeKeeper to redesign their entire website. I was in charge of redesigning the three main pages: home, contact, and the team page. After creating a uniform look for the website, I created a style guide that could be used site wide.
