About Optyn

During my 2013 summer internship at the startup-accelerator company, gener8tor, I worked with Optyn on several different projects. The first projects I was assigned to was redesigning their homepage, focusing heavily on the consumer side of the site rather than the merchant side. The result was a single-page web design that showcased the company's main features for the consumers, while also giving merchants information on pricing and a place to sign up.
