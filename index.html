Philosophy

Hello, I am Gavin Anthony. I have a passion for building products that people find both pleasing and intuitive to use. I have been designing applications (web/mobile) for over 4 years and have a good understanding of what makes design successful.

Over the years, starting in front/back end development and switching my focus solely towards design, I have learned that design should be transparent. I am a minimalist when it comes to design and I believe it should not get in the way of a user’s experience. In the end, it’s all about creating an enjoyable experience and that is my main goal.

What I do

iOS Design

iOS is probably of the most successful platforms for publishing mobile apps to. With super clear guidelines, consistency is very important to me. Every app I work on fits in very well on iOS because I always design off of style guides.

Material Design

Although much newer than the iOS guidelines, Android’s new material design goes very in depth. I have a deep understanding of Google’s new guidelines and can design for as well as translate iOS designs.

Identity Design

A company’s most important thing is its identity. No matter what platform you are on, the identity is always consistent. I strive to create unique and inspiring identities for companies that I work for.
